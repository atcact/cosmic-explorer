About 3D Solar System Explorer

Welcome to the 3D Solar System Explorer, an educational web app developed as part of Hack at Brown 2024. Explore the wonders of our solar system in 3D, click on planets for detailed information, and test your knowledge with our interactive quizzes.

Features

  • Interactive 3D Solar System Viewer
  • Click on Planets for Detailed Information
  • Educational Quizzes for Each Planet

How to Use

Simply navigate through the app using the links in the navbar. Explore the 3D solar system, click on planets, and enjoy the educational features provided. Have fun learning about our amazing universe!
