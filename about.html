About Cosmic Explorer

Welcome to the Cosmic Explorer, an educational web app developed as part of Hack at Brown 2024. Explore the wonders of our solar system in 3D, click on planets for detailed information, and test your knowledge with our interactive quizzes.

How to Use

Simply navigate through the app using the links in the navbar. Explore the 3D solar system, click on planets, and enjoy the educational features provided. Have fun learning about our amazing universe!
